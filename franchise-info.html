Frequently Asked Questions

What is so unique about Sizzlly Chicken? add

As a corporate we care about the quality, taste and store ambiance in serious manner. Our Chief Chefs back in the corporate kitchen crafts every key ingredient with the delicious and handpicked herbs and spices. This makes the Sizzlly Chicken stores so unique.

Are the meats used at Sizzlly Chicken processed? add

No, the meats used in our recipes are normally handpicked from the butcher house on daily basis.

How to reach the franchisor to find about the franchise program? add

Like minded entrepreneurs can reach our executives on +91-9790920501 or fill the form given in the Sizzlly website. Upon receiving the information, our executives will revert the customers in call with detailed on boarding procedures.

What are the options does the franchise have if they wish to own their Sizzlly Chicken outlet? add

Sizzlly Chicken franchise is offered in three variants, they are named Gold, Platinum and Diamond. Our Business experts will explain over the call.

What is the minimum space needed to operate the Sizzlly Chicken’s franchise stores? add

The minimum requirement to operate the franchise stores is 150 SQFT. However the store size can be scaled up according to the local market demand.

Does franchise owners need serious experience to manage the stores? add

No, a person without any expertise in restaurant industry can run the stores easily. Operation and maintenance of our stores are easy. Since our unique POS(Billing) and Inventory software play the key role in managing the stores without any hurdles.

What sought of supports and training does the franchise avail from the Sizzlly Chicken?add

Sizzlly Chicken will provide the end to end support beginning from the selection of store location, designs for the outlet interior and exterior, Kitchen training, opening promotion for the store, 360 Deg brand promotion, training for the day to day operation, inventory and maintenance. More importantly 365 days after sales support will be provided.

What about the franchise location rights?add

The franchise location is depends upon the location. The location rights are classified into three categories. The categories are Tier-1(Metropolitan cities), Tier-2(City) and Tier -3(Town and Panchayats). The location rights for the Tier-1 are 2 Kms radius, Tier -2 is 3 km radius and Tier-3 is town/Panchayts at 4 km radius.
